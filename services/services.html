What We Offer

Track My Car

BLOCKCHAIN meets AUTO

Car Details

Our blockchain platform securely stores and manages all your car details,
including the car company, model, date of booking, delivery, invoice, dealer name, and address.
This ensures that all data is immutable and easily accessible.

KYO: Know Your OEMs

With blockchain, you can effortlessly track and verify your car's original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).
From engine suppliers to tier suppliers, interior suppliers to stereo suppliers,
our system provides a comprehensive and transparent view of all contributors.

Car service/repair history

Maintain a tamper-proof record of your car's service and repair history.
Our blockchain solution ensures that every service and repair entry is secure,
providing you with a reliable and accessible history.

Car insurance records

Blockchain enhances the efficiency and security of managing car insurance records and policies.
Store your insurance details securely and ensure quick and reliable access when needed,
reducing fraud and speeding up claims processing.

With greater power comes greater responsibility

Track and verify your car's pollution control checks and certifications on the blockchain.
This ensures compliance with regulations and helps in maintaining an eco-friendly vehicle.
